Documentary photography

Documentary photography sheds light on social and historical issues with the intention of narrating and reproducing events, circumstances and issues through its images.

What it proposes to do is to put together different pieces of a jigsaw puzzle that, once joined together, make up the everyday life of the phenomenon it is intended to describe. It is a call to action and to act. For this work, research is indispensable. And it is only by digging deep, waiting and observing that we get closer to our subjects, that we understand their fears, their dynamics, their breath. Documentary photography is a dance in which we ask the person in front of us to perform, to put his/her life on the line in front of a system of mirrors. And what we catch is his/her reflection, his/her colours and shapes. Shapes that tell stories.
